In-Depth Overview of Sponsorship Opportunities

Welcome to the comprehensive guide to our sponsorship tiers at UAP Manila Corinthian Chapter. Each tier offers unique benefits and prime logo placements across our website and events.

Platinum Sponsorship

Our Platinum sponsors receive the highest level of visibility and engagement opportunities. They are featured prominently throughout our website and at all major events. Platinum sponsors enjoy year-round exposure and multiple speaking opportunities at our General Membership Meetings.
