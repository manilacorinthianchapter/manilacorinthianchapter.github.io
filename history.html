Our History

Key milestones of the Manila Corinthian Chapter since 1989.

  • 1989
  • 1995
  • 2002
  • 2010
  • 2018
  • 2025

1989 – Chapter Reestablishment

A renewed group of architects formally reestablished the Manila Corinthian Chapter to advance professional standards and community outreach.

1995 – First Annual Symposium

Hosted the inaugural Architecture Symposium, bringing together local and international speakers to discuss sustainable urban development.

2002 – Heritage Conservation Initiative

Launched a citywide campaign to document and restore heritage buildings in Intramuros and Binondo, in partnership with the National Commission for Culture and the Arts.

2010 – Digital Design Awards

Introduced the Digital Design Awards to recognize innovative use of technology in architectural presentations and 3D modeling.

2018 – Youth Mentorship Program

Initiated a mentorship program pairing seasoned architects with students and recent graduates to foster skills development and networking.

2025 – Virtual Collaboration Platform

Launched an online platform for members to collaborate on projects, share resources, and attend virtual events.
