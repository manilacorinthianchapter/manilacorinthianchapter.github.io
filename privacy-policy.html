Privacy Policy

We, the UAP Manila Corinthian Chapter (“we”, “our”, or “us”), are committed to handling your personal data with respect, discretion, and care. This Privacy Policy explains how we manage your information in line with our chapter operations and applicable laws.

1. What We Collect

We collect only the essential personal information voluntarily provided by members through this website—such as your name, email, PRC license number, company, and payment history.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used strictly to manage your membership account, confirm your identity, support internal records, and improve chapter services. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third-party marketing services.

3. Cookies & Consent

This website uses basic cookies to keep you logged in and ensure smooth functionality. By clicking “I Accept” or continuing to use this site, you acknowledge and accept our use of these tools.

4. How We Protect Your Data

All data is stored securely using industry-recognized platforms (e.g., Supabase), with controlled access and technical safeguards. While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no online system is completely immune to risk.

5. Your Rights

If applicable under the Philippine Data Privacy Act, you may request access to or correction of your data. We reserve the right to assess such requests based on reasonableness, data integrity, and operational feasibility.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about your information, you may reach us at:
Email: manilacorinthianchapter@gmail.com

7. NPC Registration

At this time, the UAP Manila Corinthian Chapter is not formally registered as a Personal Information Controller (PIC) with the National Privacy Commission. However, we voluntarily align with its principles and strive to uphold your data rights responsibly.

8. Limitation of Liability

This policy is informational and does not serve as a legal contract. While we aim to protect user data, we cannot be held liable for unforeseen technical errors, cyber threats, or unauthorized access beyond our reasonable control.

9. Policy Updates

This policy may be revised at any time without prior notice. Continued use of the site implies your acceptance of the most recent version.

Last updated: August 2025

Gumagamit ang site ng simpleng cookies para sa login at performance. Sa pagpindot ng “I Accept” o pagpapatuloy sa site, sumasang-ayon kayo sa paggamit ng mga cookies na ito.

Lahat ng datos ay nakaimbak sa secured platforms gaya ng Supabase. Gumagamit kami ng teknikal at administratibong hakbang para sa seguridad, ngunit hindi ganap na walang panganib ang anumang online system.

Batay sa RA 10173, maaaring humiling ng access o pagbabago ng datos. Gayunpaman, may karapatan kaming tumanggi kung labis, paulit-ulit, o hindi makatwiran ang kahilingan.
