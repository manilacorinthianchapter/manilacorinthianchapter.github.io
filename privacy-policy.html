Privacy Policy

The UAP Manila Corinthian Chapter ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and store your information through our official website.

1. Information We Collect
  • Personal identifiers (e.g., full name, email address, contact number)
  • Professional credentials (e.g., PRC license number, company, position)
  • Membership and payment data
2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely for official chapter communications, membership verification, payment tracking, and access to member-only content. We do not sell or share your data with third-party advertisers.

3. Consent & Cookies

By clicking "I Accept" on the consent banner, you consent to our use of cookies and collection of non-sensitive personal data. Cookies are used only to enhance your user experience and maintain session state.

4. Data Protection & Retention

All personal data is stored securely via Supabase with encryption and access control. Data is retained only as long as necessary for legitimate chapter operations.

5. Your Rights

In compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173), you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. Please contact us for any requests.

6. Contact

For questions or concerns, contact us at:
Email: manilacorinthianchapter@gmail.com

7. NPC Registration

As of now, the UAP Manila Corinthian Chapter is not registered with the National Privacy Commission as a Personal Information Controller (PIC). However, we remain committed to protecting your personal data and implementing reasonable safeguards aligned with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173).
