How to Become a Member

  1. Visit our Sign Up page and complete the application form.
  2. Submit required documents such as a valid ID and proof of professional affiliation.
  3. Pay the membership fee through our secure payment gateway.
  4. Await confirmation and approval from the UAP Manila Corinthian Chapter.
  5. Receive your membership credentials and enjoy member benefits.

Questions? Email us at support@uapmcc.ph
