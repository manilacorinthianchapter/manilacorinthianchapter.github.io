The MCC Scholarship Fund grants ₱10,000 per semester plus mentorship to deserving 3rd‑ or 4th‑year BS Architecture students from public universities in Metro Manila.
  • Minimum GWA: 2.5 (80%).
  • Endorsement from the dean or UAP student‑chapter adviser.
  • Proof of financial need.
Call for applications: announced every November; interviews in January; awards released before the summer term.
