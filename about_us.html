A Message from the Chapter President

Ar. Karl Vincent Santos, uap, rmp, mmep, earoph

My dear Corinthian Family,

Every chapter of our journey as architects tells a story. For the UAP Manila Corinthian Chapter, this year begins a new one — a story of connection, empowerment, and shared purpose.

When I first envisioned this digital home, I imagined a space where every Corinthian feels seen, heard, and inspired. A place where our collective energy and passion could come alive — not just during our meetings and events, but every day, in every interaction, online and beyond.

I also foresaw this platform as a way to make our chapter life easier and more sustainable — streamlining our membership drive, renewal processes, and our usual chapter transactions. By making these services more convenient for every member and less tedious for the people behind them, we are building a system that allows us to focus more on what truly matters: connection, service, and growth.

Our journey this year revolves around three powerful movements — our flagship programs that reflect who we are and what we aspire to become:

First, there is UPLINK.– UAP Integration and Alignment Program
UPLINK is our lifeline — our commitment to stay connected with UAP National, to amplify our presence in the profession, and to strengthen the bonds that make us a true Corinthian family.

Then comes ACTIVATE. – Active Lifestyle and CSR Program
I believe that a thriving chapter is a chapter that moves — together. ACTIVATE is our call to live actively, give generously, and engage wholeheartedly. From wellness and sports to community and social responsibility programs, it is our reminder that the heart of a Corinthian beats not just for architecture, but also for life and for others.

And finally, PURSUIT. – Professional Development Program
As architects, we are lifelong learners. PURSUIT is our promise to ourselves — to grow, to specialize, and to stay future-ready. Through seminars, trainings, and certifications, we are equipping every Corinthian to rise above challenges, embrace opportunities, and stand tall as proud professionals in a rapidly changing world.

This website is more than a tool — it is the living page of our story. Here, you can renew your membership with ease, discover programs that reflect your values, join initiatives that strengthen our chapter, and celebrate milestones with fellow members who share your passion. Most of all, it is a space where the Corinthian spirit thrives — a spirit of unity, service, and excellence.

As we embark on this new chapter, I invite you to UPLINK, ACTIVATE, and PURSUE with us. Together, let’s continue to grow, to serve, and to live out the Corinthian spirit with pride.

Welcome to our digital home. Welcome to our shared story.

Welcome home, my Corinthian family.



With pride and gratitude,

Ar. Karl Vincent Santos, uap, rmp, mmep, earoph
Chapter President, FY 2025–2026
UAP Manila Corinthian Chapter

Mission

In order for the UAP members to unite the registered and licensed Filipino architects in the Philippines, promote the aesthetic, scientific, and business practice of architecture; advance architectural standards of continuing education, training, and practice; and build relationships among clients, the construction industry, and architects, the following mission is adopted:

The UAP, as the integrated and accredited professional organization of architects, is the vanguard of the architecture profession in the country. It shall serve as the catalyst of dynamism and unity among Filipino architects, promoting the highest ethical conduct, discipline, and responsibility, and values beneficial to man and society, culture, and the environment in the delivery of its professional mandate for local and global practice.

Vision

UAP believes that an important purpose of architecture is to create a vision that improves the built environment. This vision is vital to the advancement of the living standards of communities and provides a service of ever-increasing value to society. Hence, the vision is:

United Architects of the Philippines (UAP): The Prime Organization of Filipino Architects.

Architect's Creed

ACCOUNTABILITY
I shall work with this general objective: that I am accountable to God, to mother earth, to my country, to my fellowmen, and to myself.

NORM OF CONDUCT
I shall uphold the ideals and follow the norms and ethical conduct of this noble profession.

PROFESSIONAL EXCELLENCE
I shall pursue moral and professional excellence to the utmost level of integrity through industrious dedication and meaningful application to my work that merits a reputation for quality of services worthy of fair remuneration.

ETHICAL RELATIONSHIP
I shall pledge myself to the highest standard of professionalism in relation to clients, colleagues, industry partners, and society.

SHARING
I shall dedicate myself to the pursuit of creative endeavor towards the goal of enlightened Art and Science, generously sharing it.

RESPECT
I shall respect the rights and works of my colleagues in the profession and confine my comments to constructive intents.

LEGACY
I shall endeavor to do my duty in the protection of our common environment and the preservation of architectural heritage for the sake of the present and future generations.

So help me God.
